Hi there!

My name is Patrick Dunn, and I'm a graduate with my bachelor's in communication from Endicott College in Beverly, MA. I'm extremely excited about breaking into the communication/film industry and currently on the lookout for a full time position to further develop my skills.

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a passionate and dedicated sports photographer and videographer based in Greater Boston. Throughout my journey as a communication major at Endicott College, I've had the incredible opportunity to combine my love for sports with my creative talents in photography and video production. This portfolio is a showcase of my work, capturing the energy, emotion, and captivating moments that define the world of sports. From high-intensity action shots to behind-the-scenes glimpses, I aim to bring the spirit of sports to life through my lens. Whether it's on the field, court, or in the stadium, I thrive in the exhilarating atmosphere of sports events, always ready to freeze that split-second of victory or determination. I invite you to explore my collection of photos and videos, which not only highlight my technical skills but also reflect my passion for storytelling and visual artistry. Thank you for visiting, and I hope my work resonates with you as much as it does with me. Let's celebrate the power of sports together!
